Glossary

Key terms related to AI, automation, and AguyanAI.

Agentic AI
Artificial intelligence systems capable of understanding goals, planning actions, and executing autonomously using available tools.
Business Process Automation (BPA)
Using technology to automate repeatable business tasks and workflows.
System Integration
Connecting different IT systems and applications so they can share data and work together.
