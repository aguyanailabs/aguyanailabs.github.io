The Autonomous Agent Core

At the heart of AguyanAI are sophisticated AI agents designed for complex integration tasks. These agents possess capabilities for:

  • Planning: Deconstructing high-level goals into executable steps.
  • Reasoning: Selecting optimal pathways, APIs, and data transformations.
  • Execution: Interacting securely with diverse systems and handling exceptions.
  • Learning & Adaptation: Monitoring performance and adjusting to changes in connected systems or APIs.

This autonomy drastically reduces manual configuration and maintenance overhead.
