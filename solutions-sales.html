How AguyanAI Boosts Sales Performance

Intelligent Lead Routing & Qualification

Agents analyze lead data (demographics, firmographics, engagement) and route instantly to the best-suited rep with full context.

Automated CRM Data Enrichment & Cleansing

Personalized Outreach Assistance

Agents can draft initial outreach emails based on templates, lead data, and recent activity, queueing them for rep review and sending.
