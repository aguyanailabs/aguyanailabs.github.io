Optimize Manufacturing Intelligence with AguyanAI

Drive efficiency, quality, and resilience on your factory floor and across your supply chain with agentic AI.

Address Critical Manufacturing Challenges

  • Managing complex supply chain logistics and disruptions.
  • Reducing costly unplanned equipment downtime.
  • Ensuring consistent product quality and minimizing defects.
  • Integrating disconnected systems (MES, ERP, SCM, PLC, IoT).
  • Optimizing production schedules amidst changing demands.
  • Improving overall equipment effectiveness (OEE).

How AguyanAI Transforms Manufacturing Operations

Predictive Maintenance Automation

Agents monitor real-time sensor data (vibration, temperature), predict potential failures using AI models, and automatically schedule maintenance orders in ERP/MES.

Intelligent Supply Chain & Inventory Management

Agents track shipments, monitor inventory levels across locations, predict demand fluctuations, identify potential delays, and automate re-ordering processes.

Automated Quality Control Monitoring

Analyze data from vision systems, sensors, and testing equipment to detect quality anomalies in real-time, flag defective batches, and adjust process parameters.

Dynamic Production Scheduling & Optimization

Agents adjust production schedules based on real-time inputs like material availability, machine status, labor constraints, and urgent order priorities.

Shop Floor Data Integration & Visibility

Connect disparate machines, PLCs, and software systems to create a unified, real-time view of manufacturing operations and KPIs.
